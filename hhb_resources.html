Retro Game Corps

Run by Russ, Retro Game Corps is a hub for enthusiasts of SBC handhelds. Russ provides high-quality, in-depth reviews, guides, and opinions on various SBC handheld devices. Whether you're seeking recommendations on what to buy or tutorials on configuring your handhelds, Retro Game Corps will offer the comprehensive resources to satisfy your needs.

r/SBCGaming

r/SBCGaming is one of the internet's largest forums for SBC gaming. It provides an outlet to discuss and share various handhelds, retro gaming adventures, and modifications of its users devices.

Retro Dodo

Retro Dodo is the world's largest retro handheld site. It keeps it users up to date with articles and videos about the latest handhelds, accessories, and more.
